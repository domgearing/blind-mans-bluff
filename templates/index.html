Blind Man's Bluff

5-player card deduction

1 Each player holds their phone to their forehead — you can see everyone else's card but not your own.
2 Take turns speaking clues about the other players' cards. When you're done, flick your wrist to pass the turn.
3 There are 3 rounds. The same person starts all 3 rounds, then the starting player rotates each new game.
4 After 3 rounds, the Final Round begins. When it's your turn, a ding will sound — tap anywhere on your screen to start recording your guess, then tap again to submit.
5 Guess your card's value and your position (Lowest → Highest). Points go to whoever guesses both correctly!
